## 1. The two business models

### Model 1 — Direct sale (CAPEX client)
- SmartGrid sells the solution to the client (client pays the quote/devis).
- Client benefits: reduced electricity invoice.
- Recurring revenue for SmartGrid: SaaS subscription + maintenance contract (hardware) + software support.

### Model 2 — Financed / revenue share (Energy-as-a-Service)
- SmartGrid finances and rents the plant ("centrale") — zero investment for the resort.
- Savings/revenue shared with client for a contract period; SaaS subscription still sold to client.
- Maintenance is SmartGrid's responsibility (plant belongs to SmartGrid).
- Land/roof lease to secure: ~30-year bail to be defined (per Boris).
- Strategic option at volume (per Boris): become an **aggregator / Responsable d'Équilibre (balance responsible party)** — flexibility/VPP revenues.
