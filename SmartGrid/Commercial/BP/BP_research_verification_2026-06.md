## 3. Spain aids — NextGen/PRTR autoconsumo (IDAE)
- RD 477/2021 programmes **closed 31 Dec 2023**. No generic autoconsumo grant open in 2026.
- Active channel: IDAE **€277.5M call (Jan 2026)** for innovative installations incl. collective self-consumption + storage; MITECO energy-communities calls (€108M). Confidence: medium-high.
- Source: pv magazine España 08/01/2026.

## 4. Italy — Transizione 5.0
- **Closed** (resources exhausted, MIMIT decree 6 Nov 2025); 2026 = wind-down only.
- Successor: **Iperammortamento 2026** — covers self-consumption PV + storage but **only EU-made modules ≥21.5% efficiency**. Procurement constraint. Confidence: high.

## 5. France / Corsica
- **Prime à l'autoconsommation ABOLISHED — arrêté 1 June 2026** (JO 4 June, in force 5 June). Surplus feed-in cut to **1.1 c€/kWh** (<500 kWc). Grandfathering for applications before 5 June 2026.
  → Self-consumed kWh now worth ~20× exported kWh: **strengthens the EMS/storage pitch**.
- ZNI/Corsica: dedicated feed-in arrêté (guichet ouvert, since 2024) with Corsica grid — **rates to verify with EDF SEI** before pricing Corsican deals. No open Corsica-specific CRE PV+storage tender found. Confidence: medium.

## 7. Regulatory drivers
- **EPBD (EU/2024/1275):** existing non-residential >500 m² → solar at major renovation/permit works by 31 Dec 2027 (from 1 Jan 2028). New non-residential >250 m²: 31 Dec 2026. New adjacent roofed car parks: 2029.
- **Loi APER ombrières:** in force but **softened** (CMP 3 July 2025: ≥50% shading of which ~35% PV ≈ 17.5% of area) and litigated. Deadlines 1 July 2026 (≥10,000 m²) / 1 July 2028 (1,500–10,000 m²). Use with caveats; EPBD is the more durable hook.
