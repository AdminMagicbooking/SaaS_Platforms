## Decision authority — what does "Lead" actually mean here?

Proposed rule (revise together):

- **The Lead can decide unilaterally on day-to-day operational matters.**
- **The Lead must consult the Backup on directional decisions** (anything
  that affects strategy, roadmap, brand, or significant cost).
- **Both co-founders must agree on**:
  - Capital allocation across products
  - Hiring above £X / year (TODO: set X)
  - Pricing changes
  - Equity, debt, fundraising
  - Strategic pivots and product wind-downs
  - Anything in `decisions-ouvertes.md`

If we disagree and cannot reach agreement, the rule is: **the Lead's call
stands, the dissent is logged in the relevant decision doc, we revisit in
30 days.** This avoids paralysis but creates an audit trail.
