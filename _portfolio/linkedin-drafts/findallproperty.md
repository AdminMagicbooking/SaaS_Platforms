# Draft

Rightmove can't tell you a listing is overpriced. Their valuation tools earn revenue from the agents listing the properties.

We're building a different thing. A free UK property tool where you paste a listing URL and get back, in seconds, an honest fair-value verdict — built on HM Land Registry sold prices, EPC floor areas, Police.uk crime data, Ofsted school ratings, Environment Agency flood maps, NHS/CQC healthcare ratings, ONS demographics. All Open Government Licence sources. No estate-agent revenue model on the consumer side, so no incentive to flatter the listing.

No scraping of Rightmove or Zoopla — when you paste a URL we fetch that one page once, read the price and postcode, discard the page. We never store portal HTML or photos.

Manchester first. Other cities follow once the model proves itself against real sold prices.

If you're an estate agent listing in our launch city: get in touch about a direct feed. Our take-rate model is built around partner agencies, not against them.
