# Draft

You approved three quotes this morning. By tonight your budget is wrong.

That's the default for most construction PMs running parallel sites — quotes get signed in one tool, budgets sit in another, and reconciliation is a Sunday-evening job. The data drifts faster than anyone can keep up with.

COREPROMA closes the gap at the source. Approve a devis, the budget recalculates immediately. Record an invoice, the corresponding budget line moves. Approve a partner-company's quote from a chantier, no double entry. The PV from the réunion de chantier is generated from your voice notes — the action items land in a hub linked to every PV, with messaging and document upload per action.

For clients who now ask about embodied carbon: every quote line is matched to an ADEME monetary intensity coefficient by Claude Haiku, with manual override and confidence scoring. Result: a one-page A–E grade card you can hand them at the end of the project.

The Pro plan covers up to 5 projects, 10 users per project, 8 construction-type step templates. €499/year. Seven-day trial, no payment taken.

Two demos, EN and FR, both interactive: https://coreproma.com
