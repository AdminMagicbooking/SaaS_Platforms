# Draft

The most useful thing an AI can say is "I'm not sure."

Most translation tools guess. They hand your staff a finished sentence in Polish or Portuguese, no warning when the legal terminology is wrong, no flag when the dialect register is off. By the time the customer replies "what does this mean?", the trust is gone.

EmailRelay does it differently. Every translation gets a confidence score. Below the threshold you set, the system refuses to send and surfaces three candidate translations with their scores, plus a one-click forward to a colleague. A glossary protects property and legal terms — "deposit", "tenancy", "section 21" — by mapping them to legally equivalent terms in the target language.

Your staff never change their workflow. They BCC the EmailRelay address on their normal CRM email. The customer gets a translated message on WhatsApp. The reply lands as an ordinary email in the inbox. Voice notes are transcribed. Photos pass through.

For the principal: a per-conversation audit trail, AML-flagged messages retained for five years, GDPR posture by design. Sold per seat plus per outbound conversation.

If your CRM still emits English to a customer who only reads on WhatsApp, the loss is silent — they don't reply, you don't know why. EmailRelay closes that loop.
