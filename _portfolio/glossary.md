## Methodology & framework vocabulary

- **BMAD** — phased product-development methodology referenced in
  FindAllProperty's spec ("BMAD Phase 1 (Analysis) complete"). Stands for
  Brief / Market / Architecture / Delivery (TODO: confirm exact expansion
  and document the phase definitions if we adopt it as a portfolio standard).
- **OGL** — UK Open Government Licence. Governs the public datasets
  FindAllProperty relies on.
- **WPML** — Waypoint Mission Markup Language (DJI's mission file format,
  used by WaypointsCreator).
- **RAMS** — Risk Assessment and Method Statement (UK construction H&S
  document, used by Jobs Tracker).
- **PV** — Procès-Verbal (French construction site-meeting minutes,
  generated by COREPROMA).
- **EPC** — Energy Performance Certificate (UK property energy rating, used
  by FindAllProperty and COREPROMA).
- **RE2020** — French environmental regulation for new buildings; basis for
  COREPROMA's carbon-grading thresholds.
- **ADEME** — French environment agency; source of the monetary carbon-
  intensity coefficients used by COREPROMA's and Jobs Tracker's carbon
  modules.

- **FieldWorker / FieldManager / ProjectManager** (Jobs Tracker) — orthogonal
