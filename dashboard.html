1. Big picture

The portfolio is 7 SaaS products grouped into 4 strategic plays. The plays do not form a single unified ecosystem — pretending they do leads to bad capital-allocation decisions. For the full narrative see _portfolio/ecosystem-vision.md.

Play 1 — Core bet

UK property ecosystem
FindAllProperty → COREPROMA → Jobs Tracker

Play 2 — Infrastructure

Cross-vertical messaging

Play 3 — Standalone bets

Vertical niches
WaypointsCreator (drones) · GTTourz (supercar tours)

Play 4 — Energy

Resort smart-grid
EcoGrid Resort (solar micro-grids)

Note on Play 3: with GTTourz added, Play 3 now holds two unrelated standalone bets. Whether to keep both, fold one, or split them into a Play 4 is an open call — see D-11 in decisions-ouvertes.md.

Objectives (proposed, awaiting Mark's reaction)

  • North star: by end of 2027, Play 1 revenue > Play 2 + Play 3 combined.
  • Play 2 and Play 3 must prove they can stand alone profitably, or be wound down.
  • Cross-product synergies (SSO, design system, hand-offs FindAllProperty → COREPROMA) are real, not aspirational.

Full vision and open strategic questions: _portfolio/ecosystem-vision.md

8. Things easy to forget

Use this section as a checklist. If you can't immediately point to where a thing is documented or who owns it, it's a knowledge gap.

Renewals & expirations

  • Domain renewals — centralised calendar of all 4+ domains
  • SSL/TLS certificates — Azure App Service handles most, but custom domains need verification
  • Stripe webhook signing key rotation — rotation schedule
  • Cesium ion token renewal (WaypointsCreator)
  • API key rotations (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google Maps, Twilio)
  • Companion app code-signing certificate (WaypointsCreator .exe)
  • Meta Business verification renewal (EmailRelay)

Onboarding the other founder / a future employee

  • Azure subscription invite + RBAC roles
  • GitHub org invite + repo access per product
  • Stripe dashboard access (read vs admin)
  • SendGrid / Migadu access
  • Anthropic / OpenAI workspace invite
  • Twilio account access (EmailRelay)
  • Domain registrar admin access
  • Demo accounts per product
  • Read ownership-roles.md (once filled)

Compliance & legal must-knows

  • ICO controller registration status per product (UK GDPR)
  • DPA / processor agreements signed with which customers
  • EU AI Act monitoring (EmailRelay translation is in scope)
  • AML evidence retention (EmailRelay, 5 years minimum)
  • Right-to-erasure (Article 17) routes per product
  • Cookie consent / tracking notice per public-facing site
  • Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy URLs per product

Disaster scenarios — do we have answers?

  • Backup strategy per Azure SQL database
  • Restore-from-backup runbook
  • Account-recovery path if Franck or Mark is unreachable
  • Customer support escalation while either founder is on holiday
  • Status page / incident communication channel

Brand & identity

  • Logo files per product — where stored?
  • Brand colour palette per product — centralised tokens or per-repo?
  • Trademark registrations — any?
  • Social media handles per product — who owns the password?
